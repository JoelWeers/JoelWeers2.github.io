Frequently Asked Questions

Can we just come in and buy Scuba Gear from you?

First you must have at least a basic Scuba License from a reputable source.

Do you rent Scuba Gear?

Yes we do rent Scuba Gear. Bring in your Scuba License and logbook and we will get you suited up. Also we may restrict which gear you can rent depending on your Scuba Log book.

Can you hand out Scuba Licenses?

We have some Scuba Instructors that you hire to train you and we will suppy the gear to get you a basic Scuba License. If that is not enough you can also sign up again to get you and advanced Scuba License.

Is it true that you are also a hat store?

Yes! We are also proudly a hat store with the finest hats you can want. Our founder was an avid hat lover and his son has kept up that section of the store in his honor.
